About Duru Tekstil

Founded in 2001, Duru Tekstil has established itself as one of the leading high-quality cotton product manufacturers in the textile industry. Our comprehensive product portfolio consists of Hydrophilic Zig-Zag Cotton, Hydrophilic Roll Cotton, Cotton Balls and Strip Cotton, each carefully crafted to meet the highest quality and performance standards.

At Duru Tekstil, we prioritize hygiene and safety in all our operations. Our products are manufactured using automatic machines that ensure they are free from contaminants. Our company holds ISO 9001:2008 and T√úV certifications.

Our company is dedicated to providing our customers with high-quality, economical products that meet their needs. We are always looking for ways to improve our products and stay at the forefront of the textile industry.

Our state-of-the-art production facility is equipped with European machinery. We continue our journey with a team of highly skilled and dedicated professionals. From sourcing our materials to packaging and shipping our products, we are committed to using sustainable and environmentally friendly practices in all our operations.
