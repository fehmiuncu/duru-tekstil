What is a Cookie?

Nowadays, almost all websites use cookies. At Duru Tekstil, we also use cookies on our website at https://www.durutekstil.com, like most websites, to provide you with a better, faster and more secure internet experience. Cookie usage is carried out in accordance with the legislation we are subject to, particularly the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698.

Cookies are small information files placed on the user's device by any website. These cookies detect when the user visits the relevant site again and make the site more suitable for the user.

This Cookie Policy aims to provide information about the processing of personal data obtained from visitors during the use of our website at https://www.durutekstil.com. It has been prepared to explain what types of cookies we use on our site, for what purposes, and how you can control these cookies.

Duru Tekstil Cookie Usage

By visiting https://www.durutekstil.com, you agree to the placement of cookies required for the use of Duru Tekstil's website on your device.

Duru Tekstil uses the information collected through cookies in accordance with the Privacy Policy.

If you do not want Duru Tekstil to place cookies on your device, you can refuse the use of cookies by selecting the "Reject Cookies" option at the bottom of your browser during your visit. However, in this case, you may not be able to use some areas of https://www.durutekstil.com properly.

Duru Tekstil reserves the right to change or discontinue the types and functions of cookies when deemed necessary. Therefore, we recommend that you regularly check the update date at the top of this Cookie Policy.

For What Purposes Are Cookies Used?

At Duru Tekstil, we only use technical cookies on https://www.durutekstil.com. The purpose of using these cookies is:

  • To increase the functionality of our website and provide ease of use.

What Types of Cookies Do We Use on Our Website?

Only technical cookies are used on the Duru Tekstil website:

Technical Cookies

Technical cookies ensure that the site works as it should. Non-working pages and areas of the site are detected thanks to these cookies and action is taken for repair. In addition, technical cookies increase the opening speed of the site and ensure faster and more efficient loading by remembering whether the user has previously logged in.

Duration of Use: Technical cookies are used for technical analysis and performance measurement purposes and are stored on the device unless the user manually clears the browser history.

What Data is Collected?

At Duru Tekstil, since we only use technical cookies on our website https://www.durutekstil.com, no personal data is collected.
