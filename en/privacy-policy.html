Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2024

At Duru Tekstil, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers and visitors who visit https://www.durutekstil.com. This Privacy Policy has been prepared to explain how your personal information is processed, with whom it is lawfully shared, and how it is protected by our company.

First and foremost, at Duru Tekstil, all information including personal data belonging to our customers and visitors is not shared with any third parties without customer consent, except for legal authorities and officials authorized to access this information in accordance with legal regulations.

During your visit to our website at https://www.durutekstil.com, your personal information is only obtained with your knowledge. Personal information recorded with your consent is protected by Duru Tekstil.

Protection of Your Information

All information about our customers and visitors is protected from unauthorized access, misuse and alteration, corruption and destruction. Your personal information (such as address, phone number, email address) will only be used to provide the service you requested and will not be shared with third parties or institutions except for legal obligations or with your permission.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites not operated by Duru Tekstil. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of those sites when you visit them. Duru Tekstil is not responsible for the privacy policies and practices of these sites.

Use of Cookies

Cookies are used on our website to improve the site usage experience of our visitors. Thanks to cookies, when you visit https://www.durutekstil.com again, we remember your previous preferences and provide you with better service. Since only technical cookies are used on our website, your personal data such as address or name is not collected.

Data Collected Through Contact Page

Name, surname and email information you submit to us through the form on our website's contact page is collected only with your consent and stored to fulfill your requests.

We would like to note that Duru Tekstil reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy. Therefore, we recommend that you check our Privacy Policy page regularly. You can track the update date from the date at the top of our page.
