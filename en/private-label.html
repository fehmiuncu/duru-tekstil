Medical Applications

Hydrophilic cotton is commonly used in medical settings for cleaning and dressing wounds. Their high absorbency makes them effective at removing debris and excess fluid from wound areas, while their soft, gentle nature makes them suitable for use on sensitive skin.

Cosmetics

One of the main benefits of using hydrophilic cotton in cosmetics is its ability to absorb and hold liquids such as creams, lotions and toners. This makes it an effective tool for applying and removing products as well as cleansing the skin.

Personal Care

Hydrophilic cotton is gentle on the skin, making it suitable for use on sensitive areas such as the face and eyes. It is also disposable, which helps maintain hygiene and reduce the risk of contamination.
